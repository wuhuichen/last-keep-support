How Emberfall handles your information

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use Emberfall, how that information is used, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

Privacy Policy

Emberfall is a portrait roguelike survival game. We aim to collect only the information needed to operate core features such as local progress, leaderboards, and in-app purchases.

1. Information We May Process

Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be processed:

  • Your chosen or auto-generated player display name for leaderboard entries.
  • Gameplay result data such as score, wave reached, hero choice, and run details.
  • A pseudonymous player identifier used to support leaderboard integrity.
  • Purchase-related information provided by Apple for in-app purchase processing.
  • Basic device or system information made available by Apple as part of app operation.
  • Local gameplay preferences and progression data stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To save gameplay settings and local run-related progress on your device.
  • To save and display leaderboard results.
  • To support in-app purchases such as Gold recharges.
  • To restore or validate access to purchased consumables when applicable.
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues you report.

3. Cloud Storage and Leaderboards

Emberfall may store leaderboard-related information using Apple's CloudKit services. This can include your player name, hero selection, score, wave, date, and a player identifier. This information is used only to operate leaderboard functionality.

4. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple may process payment, billing, and transaction information according to Apple's own privacy policies and terms.

5. Third Parties

Emberfall relies on Apple services for certain core functions, including CloudKit and StoreKit. We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the current version of the app.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate the app, maintain leaderboard records, support purchases, and comply with legal obligations where applicable.

7. Children's Privacy

If you believe a child has provided personal information in connection with Emberfall, please contact us and we will review the request.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or a privacy-related request, contact: wuhuichen009@163.com
